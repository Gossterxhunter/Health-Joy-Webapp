Physiotherapy Exercises

  • Ankle Pumps: Move your foot up and down 10 times.
  • Heel Slides: Slide your heel towards your buttocks and back 10 times.
  • Quadriceps Sets: Tighten thigh muscles and hold for 5 seconds, repeat 10 times.
  • Gluteal Sets: Squeeze buttocks and hold for 5 seconds, repeat 10 times.
  • Arm Raises: Raise your arms above your head slowly 10 times.
  • Neck Rotations: Slowly turn your head left and right 10 times.
  • Deep Breathing: Take deep breaths in and out for 5 minutes.
